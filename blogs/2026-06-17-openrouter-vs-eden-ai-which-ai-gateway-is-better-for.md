OpenRouter and Eden AI are both AI gateways serving different needs: OpenRouter is strong for LLM-only use cases, while Eden AI targets production teams needing broader AI capabilities including OCR, speech-to-text, and vision. Both charge a 5.5% platform fee, though Eden AI applies it only on actual API usage rather than upfront credit purchases.
