Using an AI API in Europe involves more than choosing the best model: developers must understand where prompts are processed, which legal entity controls the data, and what safeguards apply. This guide compares OpenAI, Claude, and Gemini, then explains three practical paths to compliance: native EU configurations, an EU-based AI gateway, or self-hosting.
